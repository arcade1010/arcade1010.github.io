Evaluations

Nike.com

The offical Nike website for all things Nike news and shopping

  • C - Clear and legible font. Simple and effective contrast
  • R - Most pages are uniform with large windows/ buttons and banners
  • A - Conents of page are alligned with grid (mostly centered banners and icons)
  • P - Closely related information is phsycially close

The website follows good CRAP principles and provides an easy-to-use interface that any potential customer could use for the first time and enjoy. Text is legible, information is display consistently and navigation is seemless.

bigbearevents.com

The website for the Big Bear Lake Oktoberfest event

  • C - Harsh contrast from background image but otherwise legible content. Jarring sidebar
  • R - Pages follow same margins and general "scaffolding" setup but content is speratic
  • A - Generally follows grid, but sidebar is a little jarring
  • P - Closely related information is generally phsycially close

The website follows some bad CRAP principles. The background images of the body and side-navigation-bar are a little jarring and information is not displayed consistently. Videos and images are placed speratically.
