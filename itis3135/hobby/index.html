History and Progression

My mom signed me up for recreational basketball leagues when I was around 10 years old. I played on rec teams for a couple years, winning a rec championship and havent played oraganized ball since. During gym class throughout middle school and highschool I knew some fundamentals but got circles ran around me by most other kids. Near the end of highschool and throughout my year in community college, I practiced almost everyday at an outside court by my old middle school. I became more consistent in my shooting, handles, driving, post-game and defending. My friends would occansionally go play full court 5v5s an the outside courts by my old high school. I'm no where near collegiate or professional level, but I can certainly hold my own in a recreational game or 1v1/2v2/3v3/4v4.
