1. Project Overview

My application will be a website displaying Information for my client's landscaping business. The inteded users will be people and organizations who need landscaping work or other general physical labor. The user will go to this website to gather information on why they should choose my client's company. Examples of previous work expierience like images and text descriptions will be displayed, along with refrences from other satisfied customers and a map of the general area my client operates in.

5. Page Design

    Home page
  • To welcome the user to the copmany's webpage
  • Users are potential or returning customers
  • Content:
    • Brief overview of the company and its mission/work
    • No interactivity on this page besides navigation
    About Us
  • To give the user a history and summary of the company and it's goals
  • Users are potential or returning customers or anyone who wants background information on the company or my client
  • Content:
    • Overview of the company and its mission/work. Provide a sense of friendliness and localness.
    • No interactivity on this page besides navigation
    Servicable area / location
  • To inform user of general operating area of my client and the company
  • Users are potential or returning customers who are unsure if the company is close enough
  • Content:
    • Description of location my client operates in. Images of locale and perhaps a map
    • Somewhere to enter a location and page does something with that information
    Experience and references
  • To show user my client's compentencies
  • Users are potential or returning customers
  • Content:
    • Images and descriptions of work done with credible references
    • Contact information or hyperlinks to contact the references.
    Why us?
  • To emphasize to user what makes this company better/different
  • Users are potential or returning customers
  • Content:
    • Description of what sets client apart and perks of doin business with this company
    • No interactivity on this page besides navigation
    Contact us
  • To give user options of contacting my client and his company
  • Users are potential or returning customers
  • Content:
    • Information displayed for email, phone, other contact methods and hours of operation
    • Text entry spaces that take a persons email/contact information and either sends an email right there or gives contact information to client

6. Dynamic Functionality of Website

  • Naviagation links
  • A calculator on location page to offer possible quoting based off distance
  • Photo gallery on Experience page
  • Interactive images on about, location, and home pages
  • Hyperlinks and text entry areas on contact page
